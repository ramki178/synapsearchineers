How do we do

Check Out Our Construction Process

01

First meet

During our first meet, we capture your requirements and record in a form and establish communication. Based on the site location, materials opted and based on design requirements we will estimate and share you the cost of construction & design.

02

Conceptulize your Idea

We will share you 2D floor drawings and cost of design and cost of construction once you we are opted us as your preferred partner of construction.
Upon your nod to go ahead further with construction, we will share you formal work agreement and 3D drawings & working drawings of your dream home.

03

Construction

Construction work will be executed by our experience civil engineers and daily/weekly progress will be shared through online channels. Our planning tools ensures we do not miss the timelines at any cost and we keep you posted on every milestone completion of the construction

04

Live your dream

After all the final works are done, you can move in to your dream home and start living your dream. We feel proud that we have shared a helping hand in achieving your goal.
